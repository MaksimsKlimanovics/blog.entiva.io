Senior Business Central architect, developer, and consultant. I help businesses modernize ERP systems, build AI-powered solutions, and integrate the kind of systems that everyone assumed were already integrated.

I've been doing this long enough to have opinions, accumulated enough war stories to fill a conference talk, and spent more hours debugging integrations than I'm willing to publicly admit.

## What I Do

My primary focus is Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central — AL development, solution architecture, SaaS migrations, API integrations, and occasionally ERP archaeology on systems older than some of my colleagues.

Beyond Business Central, I build AI agents and automation solutions for real business problems. Less chatbot, more "a system that actually does the work."

## AI Projects

**Fiona** — AI service advisor for automotive workshops. Helps service teams communicate with customers and manage jobs intelligently.

**Anatoly** — AI purchasing assistant. Makes procurement less painful.

**Anna** — AI accounting assistant for Entiva. OCR, Peppol e-invoicing, tax compliance, and a general attitude that bookkeeping shouldn't require a specialist degree.
